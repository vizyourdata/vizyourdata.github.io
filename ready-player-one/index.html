001 Ready Player One · 2018

The EggHunt

Spielberg's OASIS isn't a world so much as a warehouse — three decades of borrowed pop culture stacked floor to ceiling. This is every cameo, every car, every Easter egg in the film, mapped two ways: who shares a scene, and how far each thing travelled to get on screen.

002 the premise

A movie that is mostly footnotes.

Ready Player One is a treasure hunt about a treasure hunt. To win control of the OASIS, players have to know the dead creator's taste down to the frame — which is Spielberg's licence to cram the screen with everything. A DeLorean with a Knight Rider grille. The Iron Giant fighting Mechagodzilla. The entire Overlook Hotel rebuilt as a level.

That makes the film a strange kind of dataset: a single text that points at the whole history of games, movies, music and toys. So I catalogued the pointers. Every reference below was placed against frame-by-frame breakdowns, then tagged with the year the thing was actually made. Two numbers per reference — when and where — turn out to be enough to draw the whole machine.

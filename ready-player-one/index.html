002 the premise

A story that is mostly footnotes.

Ready Player One is a treasure hunt about a treasure hunt. To win control of the OASIS, players have to know the dead creator's taste down to the frame — licence to cram in everything. A DeLorean with a Knight Rider grille. The Iron Giant fighting Mechagodzilla. An entire Stephen King hotel rebuilt as a level.

And it happens twice. Cline's 2011 novel and Spielberg's 2018 film tell the same story but point at different references — the film swapped most of the three Gates and the final-battle robots wholesale. So I catalogued both versions, hung every reference on the story beats they share, and tagged when each thing was made. Use the Film · Novel · Both toggle to slide between them and watch the swaps.
